What really is interaction design & development program about ?



Interaction Design is the mixture of Human-Computer Interaction, Industrial Design, and User Experience Design. It is about how to make or develop the products, mobile apps, websites, and devices to meet users/consumers’ satisfaction, and still make profit for the business. The products with good Interaction Design will have: Clear instruction & guideline; Interesting and easy to understand interface; efficient without complexity; and profitable result. For example, when I design a house, I need to think about all aspects such as layout, lighting, air circulation, color theme, and furniture. The entrance door must be designed in a way that when people visit the house, they don’t confuse it with the exit door or other doors. The lighting, color and furniture arrangement need to create the comfortable feeling for visitors and make them want to visit the house many more times. The house represents for the product, the business, the website; and the visitors are the clients, users, or consumers. The difficulty in Interaction Design is to balance the client’s needs and business’s needs. Sometimes, the users/consumers/clients don’t even know what they want. Therefore, if a interaction designer follow exactly the users/consumers/clients’ reaction or opinion, it may create the bad outcome for the business.
